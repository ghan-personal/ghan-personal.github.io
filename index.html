Boring Bio

¯\_(ツ)_/¯ Gary Han, a Junior at Syosset High School in Long Island, New York, currently studies viola under the guidance of Yi-Fang Huang at Juilliard Pre-College, having previously trained with Jocelin Pan at the Juilliard School Music Advancement Program. Gary is a National YoungArts Winner and a Radda International Competition top prize recipient.

Gary is an avid physics enthusiast and researcher, having worked on Hydrogen Fuel Cell Research with Binghamton University. He has recently shifted gears to computational astronomy and is continuing his research journey there.

Goal of this Website

It has been 20+ yrs since social media has started revolutionizing what we know abt tech and communication. Yet, I find it hard to create a space for sharing projects in an organized manner. The Gen-Z apps all lack compatibility and structure while other systems (ex. Dropbox and whatever) feel too rigid. Thus, I created this website to post my projects, a few thoughts to consider, and whatever other stuff I happen to be working on. Follow along!
